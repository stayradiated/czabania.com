Instant Film: Mt. Eden 2

Sep 13, 2017

I’ve had a cold for the last couple of days and have been stuck inside with chicken noodle soup, lemon+honey drinks and a nose that doesn’t seem to stop sneezing - I considered trying for the world record of longest sneezing fit but the current world record is 977 days!

Instead I decided to venture outside for some fresh air, and ended up finding a hidden path to get up the side of Mt Eden and into Tahaki Reserve.

I decided to get some photos of how I setup the instant film camera - because sometimes the setup can be quite interesting to see.

Note that I am quite weary of the camera falling too it’s peril - so I have attached the strap to a nearby plant.

I had to be quite careful where I stepped as the grass was a little wet and it was quite a steep drop below the rocks.

I am using the Manfrotto Pixi Mini tripod - which is perfect for this camera.
